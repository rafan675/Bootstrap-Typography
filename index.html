My Hometown.

My Hometown is Kushtia. Kushtia, a charming town nestled in western Bangladesh, is known for its rich cultural heritage and fertile lands. The town, once a part of Nadia district, has a history dating back centuries. It's a hub for education, with renowned institutions like Kushtia Government University College. With its serene atmosphere and friendly locals, Kushtia offers a peaceful escape from the bustling city life.
